About Salt Lake Temple

Here are some interesting facts about the Salt Lake Temlple:

  • It was the fourth temple built in Utah (though its construction was started first) and the first built in the Salt Lake Valley.
  • The Salt Lake Temple was the only temple dedicated by President Wilford Woodruff.
  • The Salt Lake Temple was the only temple dedicated by President Wilford Woodruff. With its distinctive spires and statue of the angel Moroni, the Salt Lake Temple is an international symbol of the Church.
  • The Salt Lake Temple is the largest temple (most square footage) of the Church.
