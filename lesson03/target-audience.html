Target Audience

Local businesses and consumers.

Personas:

Bill: Bill has lived in Billy, Ohio for several years. He wants to be able to help and connect with local businesses.

Anne: Anne just recently moved to Billy, Ohio. She is wanting to open a small business and is wanting to network with local businesses.

Scenarios

1. Anne opens of the Billy Chamber of Commerce website to join it.

2. Anne is looking for contact information for a local supplier for her business.

3. Anne looks into the discovery page trying to find out more about her city.

4. Bill navigates to a business directory to see local busine in the city.

5. Bill looks for contact information for a local business.
